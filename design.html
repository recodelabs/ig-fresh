ig-fresh — pick a look

Font pairings and color palettes for the redesigned IG, each applied to the same little ICR profile-page mockup so you can judge them in context. Every palette drops the off-yellow: the draft banner and page tint move to cooler / neutral treatments. Cards marked RECOMMENDED are my picks — but this is your call.
